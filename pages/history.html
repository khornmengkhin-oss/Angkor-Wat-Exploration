Temple timeline

Built for kings, carried by culture

Angkor Wat began as a Hindu temple, later became a Buddhist site, and remains a powerful symbol of Khmer identity.

1

12th century

King Suryavarman II commissioned Angkor Wat as a grand temple dedicated to Vishnu.

2

Religious change

Over time, the temple became an important Buddhist place of worship and pilgrimage.

3

Modern symbol

Today, Angkor Wat appears on Cambodia's flag and welcomes visitors from around the world.
